RoK Betrayal Tracker

Quickly Identify Backstabbing Kingdoms

Why This Tool Exists

This extension was developed in response to our kingdom being betrayed twice in one KvK, and in two different KvKs within a year where our Star was stolen by initial allies. Hopefully this tool will allow you to identify problematic kingdoms in your matchups and adapt accordingly. Unless we make backstabbing kingdoms true pariahs, it'll continue. Lilith cannot be trusted to do the right thing in providing fair gameplay conditions when their primary goal is revenue generation. And betrayals over Stars tend to prolong fighting and thus spending.

Backstabber General Playbook

Be highly vigilant if an ally exhibits these common warning signs during matchmaking and KvK:

  • Vague Agreement: They intentionally keep initial diplomatic terms ambiguous. They will avoid committing to hard numbers, explicit DKP mechanisms, specific DKP scanning websites, or exact scan cutoff events.
    • The Objective: This allows them to later weaponize and question the "quality" of your camp's DKP (e.g., claiming your stats were padded against weaker targets or that your combat was intentionally prolonged for suspicious reasons).
    • The Pivot: If your camp loses a zone, they will cite that failure to propose that the DKP metric be ignored entirely. Settle all tracking mechanisms explicitly in initial diplomacy.
  • The Kingsland Showdown: Right before or immediately after securing Kingsland, they will force an alternative arrangement discussion. They frequently propose a "friendly fight in Kingsland"—a tactic specifically chosen when they know your troops have been heavily drained in prior frontline skirmishes.
  • Secret Backchannel: If these diplomatic conversations have begun with your initial allies, assume they have already opened negotiations with an opposing camp to sell you out and have them take your spot in the reward distribution.
  • Your Decision: When the trap springs, leadership faces a binary choice: accept inferior rewards and report the incident to community databases later, or stand your ground, fight the betrayal, and risk losing all KvK rewards entirely.
